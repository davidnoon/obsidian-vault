# OVERVIEW OF HO CHI MINH CITY PETROLEUM ONE MEMBER COMPANY LIMITED (SAIGON PETRO)

## 1. General Introduction

**Ho Chi Minh City Petroleum One Member Company Limited (Saigon Petro)** is a Party-owned economic enterprise with 100% charter capital held by the Ho Chi Minh City Party Committee.

- **Predecessor:** Petroleum Processing Joint Venture Enterprise, established on June 19, 1986.
    
- **Legal History:** * December 22, 1993: Formally established as Ho Chi Minh City Petroleum Company via Decision No. 53/QD-UB.
    
    - January 10, 2005: Converted to a One Member Company Limited via Decision No. 117/QD-UB.
        
- **Official Name (Vietnamese):** CÔNG TY TNHH MỘT THÀNH VIÊN DẦU KHÍ THÀNH PHỐ HỒ CHÍ MINH.
    
- **Official Name (English):** HOCHIMINH CITY PETROLEUM COMPANY LIMITED.
    
- **Abbreviation:** SAIGON PETRO CO., LTD.
    
- **Charter Capital:** 1,000,000,000,000 VND (One trillion Vietnamese Dong).
    
- **Headquarters:** 27 Nguyen Thong, Vo Thi Sau Ward, District 3, Ho Chi Minh City.

## 3. Major Facilities

Over 39 years, Saigon Petro has established a reputation based on "Prestige – Quality – Continuous Development." Key assets include:

- **Cat Lai Terminal & Refinery:** 26 hectares in Thu Duc City, HCMC.
    
    - **Jetties:** Two jetties (A & B) capable of receiving 32,000 DWT vessels.
        
    - **Storage:** Pipeline system and tanks with a total capacity of over 240,000 m³.
        
    - **Others:** Two road-based petroleum filling stations, a quality control (KCS) laboratory, and modern fire-fighting/utility systems.
        
- **Cat Lai LPG Plant:** Located in Thu Duc City, HCMC.
    
    - **Storage:** 3,000 tons of LPG.
        
    - **Facilities:** Bottling station, tank truck filling station, and a cylinder maintenance/inspection workshop.
        
- **Tra Noc Petroleum & LPG Terminal (Can Tho):** * **Storage:** 33,000 m³ for petroleum and 1,400 tons for LPG.
    
    - **Jetty:** Capable of receiving 15,000 DWT vessels.
        
- **LPG Filling Stations:** 07 stations with a capacity of 40 tons/day each, located in Dong Nai, Binh Phuoc, Binh Thuan, Dak Lak, Tien Giang, Dong Thap, and Bac Lieu.
    
- **Company-Owned Retail Stations:** 02 stations in Dong Nai/Binh Phuoc and 04 stations in Ho Chi Minh City.
    

## 4. Organizational Model

- **Owner:** City Party Committee.
    
- **Representing Agency:** Office of the City Party Committee.
    
- **Direct Representative:** Members of the Members' Council.
    
- **Corporate Structure:** 8 Staff Departments, 2 Business Locations, 18 Branches, 2 Subsidiaries, and 7 Associate Companies.
    

### Key Departments:

1. **Sales Dept:** Manages petroleum business operations.
    
2. **Gas Sales Dept:** Manages LPG business operations.
    
3. **Retail Management Dept:** Develops the retail network and operates existing stations.
    
4. **Finance & Accounting Dept:** Financial management and accounting.
    
5. **HR, Legal & Admin Dept:** HR, administration, ISO standards, and legal counsel.
    
6. **Technical & Production Dept:** Technical management and production oversight.
    
7. **Supplies & Capital Construction Dept:** Manages construction, repairs, equipment procurement, and land assets.
    
8. **IT Dept:** Manages information resources and digital application.
