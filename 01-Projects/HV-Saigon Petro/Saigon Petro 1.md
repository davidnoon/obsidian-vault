### **I. Current Status Assessment & Strategic Opportunities**

In-depth analysis and strategic orientation for Saigon Petro to become a leading petroleum enterprise, aligned with modern requirements:

Saigon Petro possesses **solid foundational advantages**: a 40-year history, a unique status under the Ho Chi Minh City Party Committee (political prestige), and strategic infrastructure including two major upstream terminal systems in Cat Lai and Can Tho. It also has two gas distribution centers in the North (Nghe Tinh) and South Central (Phu Yen) regions, a wide distribution network across the South, and stable, effective business results.

However, the **"traditional management system"** is the biggest bottleneck, potentially hindering development speed and the ability to meet major national objectives: double-digit growth, digital transformation, and green transformation.

The opportunities to position Saigon Petro as a key industry leader are clear:

- **B2B Market (Business-to-Business):** Supplying raw materials for aviation units (Skypec, Tapetco) — a stable and high-quality market.
    
- **B2C Market (Business-to-Consumer):** Developing high-standard hybrid energy stations combined with convenience utilities, creating a model customer experience.
    
- **Wholesale & Energy Infrastructure:** Capitalizing on urbanization, the boom in private vehicles (both gas and electric), and highway expansion to supply fuel for transit stations, transport enterprises, and construction projects.
    
- **Future Energy:** Leading trends with green products such as E10 biofuel, Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF), natural gas (CNG, LNG), and eventually renewable energy and hydrogen.
    

### **II. Reform Roadmap & Breakthrough Development Outline**

The company requires a parallel roadmap: **"Management Modernization"** to build momentum and **"Business Breakthrough"** to achieve rapid growth.

**Phase 1: Foundational Restructuring & Management Modernization (6-12 months)**

- **Overall Strategic Consulting:** Redefining the vision and mission to become the "Leading Energy Group in the South, a pioneer in digital and green transformation."
    
- **Leadership Coaching:** Programs for the Board of Directors and management levels on high-speed mindset, strong determination, and breakthrough creativity; effective state-owned enterprise management in a market economy.
    
- **Internal Process Digitization (Back-office):**
    
    - Deploying an integrated ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system for finance, accounting, HR, and logistics.
        
    - Digitizing approval processes and reporting to reduce paperwork and accelerate decision-making.
        
    - Building a centralized Data Warehouse.
        

**Phase 2: Business Model Breakthrough & Service Digitization (12-24 months)**

- **Smart Retail Channel Development:**
    
    - Service brand building: Training station staff based on world-class customer service models.
        
    - Customer experience digitization: Saigon Petro mobile app for loyalty points, cashless payments, maintenance scheduling, and convenience shopping. Implementing license plate recognition at pumps to collect consumer data.
        
    - Expanding the "Model Convenience Store" model: Combining fuel retail with cafe services, fast food, quick shopping, and electric charging points (future-proofing).
        
- **Wholesale & Logistics Optimization:**
    
    - Warehouse Management System (WMS) consulting: Optimizing cargo flow from Cat Lai and Can Tho ports to agents.
        
    - B2B Portal Development: Allowing wholesale partners to place orders, track shipments, and manage debts online.
        
    - Using data for regional demand forecasting to support import and distribution decisions.
        

**Phase 3: Pioneering Green Transformation & Scaling Tature (24-36 months onwards)**

- **In-depth New Energy Training:** Coaching technical and sales teams on technologies and markets for E10, SAF, CNG/LNG, electric batteries, and hydrogen.
    
- **Green Product Strategy Consulting:**
    
    - _Short-term:_ Promoting E10 distribution nationwide. Researching SAF supply for Skypec/Tapetco.
        
    - _Medium-term:_ Developing CNG stations for city bus fleets, taxis, and transport. Effectively utilizing existing gas distribution centers.
        
    - _Long-term:_ Researching the roadmap for renewable energy value chains (rooftop solar at stations/warehouses) and pilot electric/hydrogen charging stations.
        
- **Building an Innovation Culture:** Internal entrepreneurship (intrapreneurship) coaching programs to encourage new ideas from all employees.
