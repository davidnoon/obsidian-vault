# Bidding Checklist: Long Thanh Ground Services (Procurement Advisory)

## 📌 Context
Long Thanh International Airport Phase 1 (25M pax/year) requires world-class ground handling, ramp services, and passenger operations. Local oligopolies (VIAGS, HGS) are at capacity, creating an opening for foreign JV partners supported by local regulatory experts.

## 📋 Phase 1: Pre-Qualification & Intelligence
- [ ] **Decree 225 Compliance:** Verify NBNS (National Bidding Network System) registration for all JV partners.
- [ ] **Local Content Mapping:** Identify specific sub-sets of ground handling (e.g., specialized wide-body ramp ops) where local providers are weak.
- [ ] **JV Structure Audit:** Ensure foreign ownership percentages align with newest Investment Law 2020/2025 updates for "Restricted" sectors.

## 🏗️ Phase 2: Technical & Operational Specs
- [ ] **EASA/IATA Standard Alignment:** Map current operational manuals to Long Thanh's "5-Star" international targets.
- [ ] **HR Scale-Up Plan:** Draft recruitment pipeline for 500+ specialized ground staff (link to our recruitment opportunity).
- [ ] **GSE Leasing Strategy:** Coordinate Ground Support Equipment imports or leasing (link to our GSE leasing opportunity).

## ⚖️ Phase 3: Regulatory & Bidding Documentation
- [ ] **Bid Bond Verification:** Coordinate with BIDV/Vietcombank for necessary financial guarantees.
- [ ] **CAAV Certification Check:** Ensure foreign partner certifications are "Fast-Track" ready via newest Civil Aviation Law amendments.
- [ ] **Environmental (CORSIA) Strategy:** Include SAF/Green Transformation components in technical proposal.
