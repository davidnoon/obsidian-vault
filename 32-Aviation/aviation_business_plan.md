## 3. SCORING FRAMEWORK & METHODOLOGY

### Six-Dimension Evaluation System

Each of the 30 opportunities is evaluated across six dimensions:

**Dimension 1: Market Size (TAM) — 20% Weighting**
- Scale: 1-10 (10 = largest addressable market)
- Measure: Total annual revenue potential in Vietnam market
- Example: MRO Services (TAM=8) = $600-900M market; Business Aviation FBO (TAM=6) = $100-200M market
- Rationale: Larger markets provide more runway for growth; smaller markets easier to saturate

**Dimension 2: Speed to Revenue — 20% Weighting**
- Scale: 1-10 (10 = fastest to first paying customer)
- Measure: Months to first revenue (target: <3 months for Phase 1 opportunities)
- Example: Pilot Recruitment (Speed=9) = 1-2 months; Airline Strategy Consulting (Speed=5) = 4-5 months
- Rationale: Rapid revenue validates concept; reduces cash burn; builds momentum

**Dimension 3: Capital Required — 20% Weighting (Inverse Scoring)**
- Scale: 1-10 (10 = minimal capital needed; 1 = high capital needed)
- Measure: Startup capital to generate first revenue
- Example: Pilot Recruitment (<$2K capital = 10 score); Cold Chain Logistics ($20-30K capital = 5 score)
- Rationale: Your consortium has limited capital; favors commission/fee models over asset-heavy services

**Dimension 4: Complexity — 15% Weighting**
- Scale: 1-10 (10 = operationally simple; 1 = highly complex)
- Measure: Ease of execution; regulatory requirements; operational challenges
- Example: Cabin Crew Recruitment (Complexity=5, simple process); Predictive Maintenance Platform (Complexity=9, highly complex)
- Rationale: Lower complexity = faster to execute; fewer execution risks

**Dimension 5: Competitive Advantage — 15% Weighting**
- Scale: 1-10 (10 = strong defensible advantage; 1 = commoditized service)
- Measure: Your unique positioning vs. competitors; relationship moat; expertise differentiation
- Example: Aircraft Leasing Advisory (Advantage=7, financing relationships); Pilot Recruitment (Advantage=6, Savie Global networks)
- Rationale: Defensible advantages create sustained margin; reduce price competition

**Dimension 6: Scalability — 10% Weighting**
- Scale: 1-10 (10 = high growth trajectory; 1 = limited scaling potential)
- Measure: Revenue growth after Year 1; unit economics improvement; market expansion
- Example: Digital Cargo Platform (Scalability=9, can serve all Vietnam airlines + regional); Airport Retail (Scalability=8, multiple locations possible)
- Rationale: Venture needs path to $1M+ revenue; scalability validates business model

### Composite Score Calculation

**Formula**: 
Composite Score = (TAM × 0.20) + (Speed × 0.20) + (Capital × 0.20) + (Complexity × 0.15) + (Competitive Advantage × 0.15) + (Scalability × 0.10)

**Range**: 5.0 (lowest) to 10.0 (highest)

**Interpretation**:
- **8.0+**: Top-tier opportunities; immediate launch
- **7.5-7.99**: Strong secondary opportunities; Phase 2 launch (April-May 2026)
- **7.0-7.49**: Viable opportunities; Phase 2-3 launch (May-July 2026)
- **6.5-6.99**: Conditional opportunities; launch if Phase 1 generates 40%+ of revenue targets
- **<6.5**: Lower priority; pursue only if core opportunities underperform
