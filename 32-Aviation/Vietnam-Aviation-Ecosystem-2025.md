# Analysis Report: Vietnam's Aviation Ecosystem Market (2025 and Beyond)

## Executive Summary
The year 2025 marks a strategic turning point for Vietnam's aviation industry, characterized by strong market recovery, formalization of service ecosystems, and the rise of the **Low-Altitude Economy (LAE)**.

## I. Market Drivers and Competitive Landscape (2025)

### 1. Strong Growth Signals
- **Passenger Output:** H1 2025 reached 41.3 million passengers (10% increase YoY). Total airport throughput hit nearly 60 million.
- **International Market:** The primary growth engine. H1 2025 reached 23 million passengers (up 13%), driven significantly by surges from South Korea and China.
- **Cargo Transport:** Strong breakthrough with 696k tons (up 14.6%). International cargo contributed 580k tons (up 17.7%). Demand forecast to increase 8% overall in 2025.
- **Ancillary Services:** High profitability reported across service enterprises:
    - **SCS:** +39% profit
    - **SAS:** +50% profit
    - **NCT:** +22.95% profit
    - **ASG:** +589.8% profit

### 2. Airline Competitive Structure
- **The Duopoly:** Vietnam Airlines Group and Vietjet Air maintain ~90% market share.
- **Market Share Breakdown (First 8 months 2025):**
    - **International:** Vietjet (23.2%) leads Vietnam Airlines (17.7%).
    - **Domestic:** Vietnam Airlines Group (48.2%) slightly leads Vietjet (44.7%).
- **New Entrants:**
    - **Sun Phu Quoc Airways (SPA):** Established June 2025, expected takeoff Q4/2025. Focused on full-service/charter models for high-end tourism.
    - **Vietravel Airlines:** Potential growth following T&T Group investment.

## II. Infrastructure & Ecosystem Development

### 1. The Long Thanh Ecosystem
- **Vision:** Position Long Thanh International Airport as the largest aviation transit hub in Southeast Asia.
- **VNA Strategy:** Vietnam Airlines acts as the core developer for high-synchronization services.
- **Progress of Key Projects (2025-2026):**
    - **VIAGS Project No. 1:** Repair/maintenance of ground equipment. Roof raising Nov 2025, completion by Dec 19, 2025.
    - **VACS Catering Factory No. 1:** Substantial completion Dec 2025, fully operational mid-2026.
    - **VAECO Maintenance Hangar No. 1:** Construction completion target Dec 19, 2025.
- **Value Chain Expansion:** VNA establishing a dedicated **Air Cargo Transport Company** in 2026.

### 2. Development of the Gia Binh Aviation Center
- **Status:** Groundbreaking occurred August 19, 2025 (Bac Ninh).
- **Positioning:** Strategic dual-use airport for the Capital Region and a "New National Icon."
- **Capacity Targets:** 
    - 2030: 30M passengers / 1.6M tons cargo.
    - 2050: 50M passengers / 2.5M tons cargo.
- **Sustainability:** Committed to **Net-Zero by 2050** and LEED Gold certification for terminal.

### 3. Integrated Group Models & Cross-Sectoral Cooperation
- **ACV & Sun Group:** Strategic agreement (June 25, 2025) to reach international 5-star terminal standards.
    - **Sun Phu Quoc Airways (SPA):** Takes off Q4/2025. Charter model synced with Sun Group's resort ecosystem.
- **T&T Group:** Integrated model revolving around T&T Airlines.
    - **Vietravel Airlines:** T&T as strategic shareholder; target 30-50 aircraft by 2030.
    - **Quang Tri Project:** 10,800ha aviation-logistics-airport urban complex (First of its kind in Vietnam).

## III. Technology Trends & Strategic Transformation

### 1. Legal Corridor: Institutional Turning Point
- **Legislative Status:** Draft amended **Vietnam Civil Aviation Law** submitted to National Assembly on October 20, 2025.
- **The "Mindset Shift":** Aims to modernize institutions, encourage private investment, and facilitate international integration.
- **New Policies:** Specifically addresses **Low-Altitude Air Transport (UAVs)** and environmental/carbon policies (CORSIA/SAF).

### 2. Low-Altitude Economy (LAE): The New Frontier
- **Strategic Positioning:** Operations below 1,000 meters (UAVs, Drones, eVTOLs). Global market projected at **$700B by 2035**.
- **The LAE Alliance:** Formed mid-October 2025.
    - **Chair:** Nguyen Van Khoa (FPT CEO).
    - **Vice-Chairs:** Don Lam (VinaCapital), Tran Anh Tuan (Vietnam UAV Network).
- **Core Goal:** Position Vietnam as a regional LAE leader.
- **Regional Pilot:** HCMC strategic agreement signed for urban LAE deployment.
- **Human Resources:** Radical growth in demand for high-quality UAV education and training.

### 3. Green Transformation & Digitalization
- **Green Pillars:**
    - **CORSIA:** Voluntary phase participation starting Jan 1, 2026.
    - **SAF (Sustainable Aviation Fuel):** VNA using SAF for all Europe departures since 2025. Binh Son Refining commercializing SAF Q4/2025.
- **Digital Infrastructure:**
    - **ACV ID:** Biometric identification system deploying in 2025.
    - **SkyBreathe:** AI/Big Data fuel optimization at Vietjet.
    - **VNA AI:** GPT-4 powered workflow streamlining at Vietnam Airlines.

## IV. Challenges & Strategic Fragility

- **Infrastructural Load:** Persistent overcrowding at Tan Son Nhat and Noi Bai hubs.
- **Financial Pressure:** SAF costs are 2-3x higher than conventional fuel. VNA projecting **$5-6M addition fuel costs in 2025** due to EU regulations.
- **Underdeveloped Supporting Industry:**
    - Only 5 airframe manufacturers and 1 engine unit currently active in Vietnam.
    - **Major Gaps:** Avionics, advanced materials, and specialized aircraft paint.
    - **Entry Barriers:** High time/cost requirement for IATA, US, and European certifications.
- **Human Resources Crises:**
    - **Demand:** Industry requires **>58,000 personnel by 2025**.
    - **Shortages:** Critical risk of pilot and high-quality technical staff shortages.
    - **Education Pivot:** Universities are accelerating partnerships with **VNA** and **VAECO** to train engineers to international **EASA-Part 66** standards.
