## About the Role

We are seeking a capable and detail-oriented Digital Forms & Documentation Specialist to support the development of branded, interactive business documentation.

This role will focus on creating structured, professional templates and fillable digital forms to support internal operations and client-facing documentation.

You will work closely with the Director to ensure documentation is clear, consistent, and aligned with professional standards.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop branded document templates incorporating logo, formatting standards, and consistent layout
    
- Create fillable PDF forms (including dropdowns, tick boxes, and signature fields)
    
- Build structured Word templates using content controls and document protection
    
- Format documentation for online completion and electronic use
    
- Ensure consistency across document suites
    
- Assist in maintaining document version control and standardisation
    

---

## Skills & Experience Required

- Advanced Microsoft Word skills (styles, structured formatting, document protection)
    
- Experience creating interactive fillable PDFs (Adobe Acrobat or equivalent)
    
- Strong formatting and layout capability
    
- High attention to detail and accuracy
    
- Ability to work independently and meet agreed timelines
    

Experience in compliance, HR, WHS, governance, or professional services environments is advantageous.
