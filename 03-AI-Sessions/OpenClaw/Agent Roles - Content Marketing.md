SCF is searching for a Marketing professional with a passion for storytelling, based in our Adelaide Head Office. We’re after someone who’s keen to use their skill, curiosity, and knowledge to reshape marketing expectations in the shipping container industry.

This role is broad, fulfilling, and just a little bit gritty. You’ll be part of a highly functional, driven, and successful Marketing team – who’s there to provide support and guidance, while also giving you room to experiment and grow. - You have an eye for detail.

- Tertiary qualifications in Marketing or Communications preferred.
    
- Completion of digital marketing subjects/courses is an advantage.
