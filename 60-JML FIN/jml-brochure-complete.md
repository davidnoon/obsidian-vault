# PAGES 8-9: HOW WE INVEST

## PAGES 8-9: HOW WE INVEST

### Section Title: "Our Investment Model: Flexible Capital for Vietnam's Development"

**Opening Narrative:**

JML Financial Services & Investment Management specializes in flexible project finance—providing patient, flexible capital, specialized expertise, and long-term partnership to opportunities that create value for all stakeholders while advancing Vietnam's sustainable development.

Our approach differs fundamentally from traditional development finance models. We don't view projects as transactions to close and exit. Instead, we build enduring partnerships—combining flexible capital structures, majority ownership governance, and multi-decade value creation horizons.

This extended engagement model enables something traditional finance cannot: the opportunity to compound value, optimize operations, and sustain community benefits throughout an asset's entire lifecycle.

**How We Work: The Four-Phase Partnership Model**

**PHASE 1: CONCEPT (Months 1-6)**

Local project partners identify opportunities aligned with Vietnam's development priorities. JML monitors market developments, provides strategic guidance, and assesses whether projects meet our three core investment criteria: development impact, commercial viability, and additionality.

We're actively engaged from day one—not as a participant yet, but as a strategic observer and advisor. When the right opportunity emerges, we're ready to move quickly.

**PHASE 2: EARLY STAGE DEVELOPMENT (Months 6-24)**

When an opportunity aligns with JML's investment thesis, we assume lead governance and deploy early-stage development capital (2-10% of total project cost). This critical period involves:

- Comprehensive due diligence across technical, financial, environmental, and social dimensions
- Stakeholder alignment and community engagement
- Detailed financial modeling and optimal capital structure design
- Regulatory approval navigation
- Securing majority equity stake or partnership agreement

Our expertise and capital reduce project risk at the earliest, highest-uncertainty stage—when traditional capital is unavailable or unwilling to invest. This is where we add irreplaceable value.

**PHASE 3: CONSTRUCTION (Months 18-42, approximately)**

We maintain governance and majority stake while overseeing construction execution. Investment partners deploy capital at or near financial close. Our active management ensures:

- Quality execution and performance standards
- Budget and schedule discipline
- Risk mitigation and issue resolution
- Community relationships and local stakeholder management
- Seamless preparation for operational transition

**PHASE 4: COMMERCIAL OPERATION & LONG-TERM VALUE CREATION (Year 3 Onwards)**

Unlike traditional models that exit post-operation, JML maintains majority ownership and active governance throughout the project's operational life. This extended horizon enables:

- Sustained value creation and operational optimization
- Flexibility to reinvest distributions for growth
- Long-term community and stakeholder relationships
- Strategic positioning for market opportunities
- Development impact delivery throughout project lifecycle
- Strategic exit optionality if compelling opportunities arise—but no exit pressure

This is where our model fundamentally diverges from development finance. We don't measure success by exits. We measure it by the enduring value we create.

**The Stakeholder Partnership Framework**

JML Financial doesn't work alone. Our success depends on productive partnerships across three stakeholder categories:

**Local Project Partners**

Vietnamese developers, entrepreneurs, and operating companies bring market knowledge, community relationships, and local execution capability. We amplify their impact through capital, institutional expertise, and governance structure—creating local value while ensuring professional standards and financial discipline.

These are the people on the ground who know their markets, their communities, and their opportunities. We provide the capital and institutional framework to turn their opportunities into enduring enterprises.

**Investment Partners & Co-Investors**

Development finance institutions, commercial lenders, and institutional investors participate from financial close onwards, providing co-investment capital and regular returns. 

The distinctive advantage: they can exit via refinancing or sale while JML maintains stewardship—enabling them to recycle capital to new opportunities while we continue long-term value creation on the assets we've built together.

This flexibility benefits everyone: co-investors can optimize their own portfolio allocation while projects benefit from stable, experienced governance.

**Technical Advisors & Specialists**

Engineering firms, environmental consultants, and specialized experts support across all project phases, ensuring technical excellence, compliance, and operational optimization.

**Five Core Principles**

Our investment approach rests on five integrated principles:

**1. FLEXIBLE CAPITAL THROUGHOUT THE LIFECYCLE**

We provide patient, adaptive capital from concept through multi-decade operation—not constrained by fixed exit timelines or rigid mandates. This flexibility is our competitive advantage.

**2. MAJORITY OWNERSHIP & STRATEGIC GOVERNANCE**

Majority equity stakes and active board participation enable long-term value optimization and operational excellence without compromise. We have skin in the game and decision-making authority to match.

**3. LOCAL PARTNERSHIP & COMMUNITY INTEGRATION**

We amplify local expertise through capital and institutional relationships while ensuring community benefits are sustained throughout project lifetime. Vietnam's success is our success.

**4. INVESTMENT PARTNER FLEXIBILITY**

Co-investors participate in financial returns without forced exit pressure. They can exit when they choose while JML continues value creation. Everyone wins.

**5. DEVELOPMENT IMPACT AS CORE INVESTMENT CRITERION**

Every investment decision integrates measurable benefits to Vietnam's economic development, affected communities, and society. This is not secondary to financial returns—it is fundamental to our investment thesis.

**Why This Model Works**

Traditional development finance works best during the highest-risk early stages. Then they exit, leaving operational challenges to others.

We believe the real value creation happens after financial close—during the years of operational excellence, market evolution, and strategic optimization. By staying engaged, we can:

- Respond to market changes and opportunities
- Invest in improvements and expansion
- Build enterprises that grow stronger over time
- Ensure community benefits are delivered consistently
- Create true long-term wealth

This is patient capital in its truest form. We invest in projects we want to hold for decades—and we structure every partnership to support that long-term vision.

**The Result**

Projects that might never have been financed become operational assets serving Vietnam's development. Co-investors realize attractive returns without operational burden. Local partners build enduring enterprises. Communities benefit from sustained investment and employment.

And JML Financial builds a portfolio of resilient, value-creating enterprises that compound returns and impact over decades.

This is how flexible capital catalyzes Vietnam's development.
